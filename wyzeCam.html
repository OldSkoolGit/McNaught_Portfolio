Wyze Cam V2

Information and Resources to Purchase and View a Wyze Cam V2 on a Desktop Computer

The Wyze Cam V2 is a low cost, fully featured Internet Protocol (IP) Camera that provides an affordable solution to home security. The Wyze Cam V2 enables users to view content captured by their camera via a Mobile App available for both iOS and Android. Although currently configured to work only with mobile devices, on this page you will find everything you need to purchase, set up and view a Wyze Cam V2 from a desktop computer. Click here for a document outlining a research project that set out to achieve this aim using the VLC Media Player.

Wyze Cam V2

The Wyze Cam V2 is available for purchase from the Wyze Cam website for a price of $USD19.99, making it one of the most affordable IP Cameras available on the market. Currently, the company will only ship to United States addresses but the website provides excellent information and resources about Wyze Cam products. The website also features a forum where users from across the world share their views and experiences about the products.
Please click on the image to the left to view the Wyze Cam V2 on the Wyze Cam website.

Purchasing the Wyze Cam V2 in Australia

Although currently not available officially outside the United States, Wyze Cam products have a strong following in Australia, as evidenced by the number of Australian contributors on the Wyze Cam website's forum. For customers outside the United States interested in Wyze Cam products, they can be purchased through various internet retailers, including Amazon.com.
Please click on the image to the left to view the Wyze Cam V2 for sale on Amazon.com.

VLC Media PLayer

The VLC Media Player is a free, open-source media player that will play just about anything you can throw at it. The VLC Media Player can be configured to enable owners of Wyze Cam products to view their camera's footage on a desktop computer.
Please click on the image to the left to download VLC Media Player from the videolan.org website.

Wyze Cam V2 Live Camera Feed

If you have purchased the Wyze Cam V2, downloaded and installed the VLC Media Player, and are connected to the same network as the camera, you will be able to view the camera's live feed on your computer.
