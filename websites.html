Website Projects

Websites I Have Created or Contributed To

TAFE Alumni

The TAFE Alumni website was created as part of an assessment in my Web Development Diploma course. The Project Team worked with a real-life client to build a website primarily aimed at current and former students of Central and North Metropolitan TAFE. The website enables members to connect and remain in contact with lecturers and other students (current and former) and keep track of where former students are and what they are doing in industry. The website also provdes a facility for those students to share their experiences.
Please click on the image to the left to view the website.

Motivation Foundation

I was part of a Project Team who worked on this website for a real-life client, the Motivation Foundation. They are a not-for-profit organisation whose purpose is the relief of poverty or distress in disadvantaged young people through education and development of life employability skills. The website launched on 31 January 2019 and has received positive feedback from both the client and visitors to the site.

Japon Car

The japoncar.com website was designed to be a repository of information and resources about Japanese cars, including the history of the Japanese motor industry, manufacturers and models they produce, Japanese cars featured in movies, buyers guides and photo galleries. The website was developed as part of an assessment for my studies in Web Development and the design was received well by lecturers and fellow students.

007 Unclassified

007 Unclassified was the second website I created and was developed as a repository for facts and information about MI6 Agent James Bond, the World's best known and most iconic Secret Agent. The website contains factoids about the films and the books, featuring information about the cars, the gadgets and the man himself, Agent 007.
